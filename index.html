About Us

Welcome to Barney's Karahiwala, where authentic Pakistani cuisine meets warm hospitality. Located in the heart of Surrey, BC, we bring you the true flavors of Pakistan through our traditional karahi dishes and tandoori specialties.


Our skilled chefs use only the finest ingredients and traditional cooking methods passed down through generations. From our sizzling karahis to our perfectly marinated BBQ items, every dish is prepared with passion and served with pride.


Whether you're craving the robust flavors of our Lahori Chicken Karahi, the tender succulence of our Lamb Chops, or the vegetarian delights of our Shahi Paneer, we have something to satisfy every palate.
